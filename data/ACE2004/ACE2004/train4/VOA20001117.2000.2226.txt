The Voice of Russia DX Club , via BBC monitoring reports that Radio Canada International , and YLE Radio Finland , are conducting test re-broadcasts in St. Petersburg Russia , on 684 KHz medium wave. RCI is in Russian and YLE in Finish, each one hour per day. The programs are downlinked from a world radio network transponder.
